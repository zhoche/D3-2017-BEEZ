Bee cool

Gardens are extremely important for bumblebees, and vice versa. Bees need flowers for sustenance, and flowers need bees for pollination. But it's important the flowers you grow provide the food bees need. You can also help your garden wildlife by creating dead wood habitats, or with our top four jobs for wildlife in autumn. Great flowers start with pollination, and for that you need bees. Find out how to get the busiest garden insects to work for you, in our feature.
